Member Benefits


When you join the Allied Health and Therapy Network, you get access to a range of business and clinical resources. We’re aiming to add to these as we grow, but here’s what we have so far.

Insurance

Get quick quotes from our insurance partner, Tapoly Insurance, for all your business and professional indemnity insurance needs.

CPD Diary

Your membership gives you a free subscription to our online CPD diary, compliant with a number of UK professional bodies’ requirements.

DBS Applications

Getting enhanced level DBS checks for yourself when you’re an independent practitioner can be difficult. Not anymore – get them done through us!

Journals

Access your 50% discounted subscription of the Journal of Allied Health.

Additionally, access our current institutional journal subscription as part of your membership at no extra cost, which currently consists of 4 health related journals (we’ll be adding more soon).

Resource Partners

We’ve teamed up with a number of resource providers who are offering our members discounts on their training and resources which can be used by a number of different professional groups.

Lifestyle Benefits

Being independent practitioners ourselves, we know running your own business is rewarding, but can be hard work.

We’re teaming up with some companies and providers to offer you discounts on their products so you can focus on yourselves too.

Networking

We want you, as an independent practitioner, to be able to make connections with other professionals in your area.

Use our member to member search engine to connect with, and support, each-other.

Recruitment

If your business is growing, you’re inevitably going to get to the point you need to recruit.

We can’t take the pain away that comes with that process. But we can offer our members fair (under 10%) recruitment fees, but only if you’re a member of AHTNetwork.

Ethical, honest recruitment with integrity, by health professionals for health professionals – AHT Recruitment (coming soon).
