About Us


Allied Health and Therapy Network Ltd is a membership organisation that brings together an important group of health and education professionals. These professionals often work closely with each-other in a multi-disciplinary way supporting people with a range of medical, educational and emotional needs.

For the Public


We want members of the public to be able to find the professionals they need all in one place. We hope this reduces the frustrations of conducting multiple searches in multiple places when they need more than one therapy to support their loved ones.

We hope our website offers you an easy way to connect with allied health, and other associated therapists, across the UK.

For Professionals


We want to provide the professionals, who register with AHTNetwork, a community of independent (private) professionals who can help each-other grow their businesses while working together in a best practice, multidisciplinary way.

Any community of private health professionals needs access to clinical information and business tools beneficial to the growth and running of their businesses. See what some of the member benefits you are entitled to when you join the AHTNetwork by searching here. We believe that working independently shouldn’t mean working in isolation.

Help Us Be Better

We know that we can always be better and one way of doing this is to listen to others’ perspectives.

If you have something to say about the website, whether you’re a member of the public searching for therapy support or a professional member – please don’t hesitate to get in touch with us. We would love to hear from you.

If you have any queries about how to use the website or how to become a member, please contact us at hello@ahtnetwork.com or check the ‘join us’ page.
